The Collatz Conjecture 📄

The Collatz Conjecture is an unsolved math problem with simple rules.

  1. Start with any number.
  2. If the number is even, half it.
  3. If the number is odd, triple it and add 1.

Here's an example of the process:

Step Number Reasoning
0 5 5 is the original number.
1 16 Since 5 is odd, It is tripled, and 1 is added to it.
2 8 16 is halved since it's even.
3 4 8 is also halved.
4 2 4 is halved.
5 1 Afier 1 is reached, it repeats in an endless loop.

Do all numbers eventually lead to 1? Despite the fact that the average third grader can understand this problem, it has stumped some of the best mathemeticians in the world for decades.
